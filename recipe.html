How to make spam fried rice

Ingredients:
  • Rice (day old rice is preferred)
  • Spam
  • Egg
  • Oyster sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar

If you are using day-old rice, heat it up in the microwave for about 1 minute before you start cooking, and make sure you portion it for one serving. All you have to do to prep is to slice your spam, about two to three slices per serving, and make your sauce. In a small sauce bowl, mix together oyster sauce, soy sauce, and a bit of sugar. Another good option is to use teriyaki sauce if you like a slightly sweeter flavor. In a pan, cook one egg however you like, whether it is scrambled, fried, or sunny-side up. Then, grill your spam for about two minutes on medium-high heat on each side until it becomes golden and slightly crispy. Once cooked, pour the sauce over the spam and make sure every piece is coated evenly. When everything is done, you can either mix the egg into the rice or place it on top, followed by the crispy and fragrant spam. This simple dish makes the perfect meal, whether you eat it with rice, egg, and spam all together, or wrap it in a piece of seaweed like a sushi roll. Add some yum yum sauce for an extra creamy bite.
